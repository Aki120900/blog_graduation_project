Week 6 – Website Setup & Formative Preparation

Published: 2025-04-18 | Tags: website, presentation, formative

With the formative review approaching, I focused this week on preparing all essential deliverables.

I finalised my SMART goals, MoSCoW prioritisation list, Gantt chart, and literature review. In addition, I gathered relevant materials such as data visualisations, Maya animation tests, and initial concept sketches. These were compiled into a single submission folder and showcased through my newly built website using Astro.

The site features dedicated sections for the simulation, research insights, data overlays, and thesis documentation. I also rehearsed my presentation outline, ensuring I could clearly communicate the connection between data and design throughout my workflow.
