Week 2 – Literature Review & Research Themes

Published: 2025-02-28 | Tags: research, literature review

This week I focused on establishing a strong theoretical foundation for the project. I reviewed sources on adaptive architecture, biomimicry, modular systems, and kinetic design.

Key references include Biomimicry in Architecture by Michael Pawlyn, and case studies like the Eastgate Centre in Zimbabwe and the Al Bahr Towers in Abu Dhabi. These will influence both the architectural behaviours and the simulation logic in the final city design.

I’ve also drafted a preliminary literature review, which will be expanded and refined for the thesis.

Overview

This literature review explores adaptive, biomimetic, and kinetic architecture, modular urban systems, data-driven simulation, and speculative design. Each theme is aligned with the concept of creating a resilient, animated city inspired by Omashu from Avatar: The Last Airbender and rigged in Maya using real seismic data.
